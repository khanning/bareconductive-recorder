How to use

NOTE: This tool is for creating ONE sound clip at a time.

1. Record your sound clip.
Each time you click the record button the previous recording will be erased. If you make a mistake, you can just click record again and it will start over with a blank recording.

2. Choose the track #.
The number corresponds to the pad on the Bare Conductive board. So track #0 will go with pad #0, track #1 will go with pad #1 and so on.

3. Download the recording.
The recording will be saved to your computer to be copied over to the Bare Conductive sdcard.

TIP: If you download multiple recordings of the same track #, you may have to delete the extra number in the filename.
e.g. TRACK001 (1).mp3, you would delete the (1).
